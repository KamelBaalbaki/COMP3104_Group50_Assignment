George Brown College is a leading institution in Toronto, Canada. 
It emphasizes applied learning, industry connections, and prepares students for real-world careers. 
The downtown campus locations allow students to be close to many industries and opportunities.
George Brown College offers over 160 full-time programs across various fields.
Founded in 1967, George Brown College has grown into one of Canada's largest colleges.
George Brown focuses on applied learning and industry partnerships.
