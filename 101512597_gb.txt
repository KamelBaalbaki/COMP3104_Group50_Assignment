George Brown College (GBC) is a public, fully accredited college of applied arts and technology located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 
It offers a wide range of programs in business, health sciences, community services, early childhood education, construction, engineering technologies, and the arts. 
The college emphasizes hands-on learning and partnerships with industry leaders, giving students strong career-oriented skills.
